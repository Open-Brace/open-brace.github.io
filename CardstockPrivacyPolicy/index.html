Privacy Policy for the Cardstock App

Last updated: 04/19/2020

We are the developers of Cardstock. This privacy policy describes how we process your personal information and which privacy rights you have when you are using Cardstock. Please contact us at the contact information below if you have any questions or comments.

1. Personal Information Collection and Use

If you grant Cardstock permission, we may collect and use personal information from you as follows.

  • Camera

Purpose: So you can scan cards into your catalog

Please note that you can decline a permission or revoke it after you granted it in the iOS settings. If you do so, we will not collect your personal information related to that permission. However, the functionality of Cardstock may be limited as a result.

We may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system.

2. Personal Information Sharing

We do not share any personal information.

3. Tracking Technologies

Neither Cardstock nor any third party services are using tracking technologies.

4. Social Logins

Cardstock does not use any social login functionality.

5. In-app Purchase Information

Cardstock does not process any in-app purchase information.

6. Privacy Rights of California Residents

Based on your selection, this Section will be omitted in the exported privacy policy.

7. Privacy Rights of Users in the European Economic Area

Based on your selection, this Section will be omitted in the exported privacy policy.

8. Children's Personal Information

Users of Cardstock must be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take all reasonable steps to delete it as soon as possible. If you become aware that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the contact information below.

9. How Long We Keep Your Personal Information

We will keep the personal information that we collect as long as it is necessary for the purposes for which we collect it.

10. How We Protect Your Personal Information

We use encryption to protect personal information stored and transmitted online. Only personnel who need the personal information to perform a specific function have access to such. The computers and servers on which we store personal information are kept in a secure environment.

11. Policy Changes

When we make material changes to this privacy policy, for example, due to app updates or changes in the law, we will post the updated policy here. The policy will always apply in the version at the time of your use of Cardstock even if you downloaded Cardstock at a time when a previous policy version was applicable.

12. Accessibility

This website conforms to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0. However, if you are having difficulties obtaining information from it, please contact us at the contact information below. We will try to make the information available to you in another format and answer any question that you may have.

13. Contact Us

Please contact us if you have any questions or concerns.
